What is a social media benchmark?



A social media benchmark is an industry standard that helps you measure your performance against similar brands.



In other words, it shows what “good” looks like for metrics like engagement, reach, and growth, so you’re not evaluating your results in a vacuum.



Social media benchmarks provide important context for your social media KPIs. While tracking your own social performance over time is valuable, benchmarks help you understand how you stack up against the broader industry and where there’s room to improve.



They also help you set realistic social media goals. Different industries perform differently on social media. For example, entertainment brands often see higher engagement than healthcare organizations. 



Knowing what’s typical for your industry helps you set targets that are ambitious, but achievable (without burning out your social team).
