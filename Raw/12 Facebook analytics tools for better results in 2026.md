Facebook gives you a ton of data. The real challenge is turning it into something useful. In this guide, we break down 12 of the top Facebook analytics tools to turn those numbers into insights.



Key takeawaysFacebook analytics tools help you track both paid and organic performance.They show how your Facebook content is performing and who it’s reaching, including audience demographics.Top tools include Meta’s Business Suite and third-party platforms like Hootsuite, Buffer, and Rival IQ.Regularly checking your Facebook analytics helps you improve results and make smarter strategy decisions.
